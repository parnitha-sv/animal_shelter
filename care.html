Harmful Foods for Dogs

  • Chocolate
  • Grapes & Raisins
  • Onions & Garlic
  • Avocados
  • Caffeine
  • Alcohol
  • Xylitol (found in sugar-free gum and candies)
  • Cooked bones (can splinter and cause internal damage)

Ways You Can Help

  • Adopt or foster a dog to provide them with a loving home.
  • Donate supplies like food, bedding, and toys to the shelter.
  • Volunteer your time to help with feeding, cleaning, and walking the dogs.
  • Help raise awareness on social media and in your community.
  • Report stray or injured dogs to local animal services or shelters.
  • Support spay and neuter programs to prevent overpopulation.

Basic Care Tips for Stray Dogs

  • Approach gently and offer food from a distance to gain trust.
  • Provide clean water and a safe, quiet place to rest.
  • Check for signs of illness or injury, and contact a vet or shelter if needed.
  • Use a leash or crate if you need to transport the dog safely.
  • Post on local lost-and-found pet groups to help reunite dogs with owners.
